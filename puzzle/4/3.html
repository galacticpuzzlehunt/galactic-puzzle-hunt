The Treasure of Apollo

Congratulations! You managed to find the core path to the Hidden Temple of Apollo! Now all you have to do is open the chest and claim the item inside. But what are these instructions? Maybe there are some clues hidden in the temple itself.

First, place your blood on the lock. We need to make sure your bloodline is powerful enough. (3)

Second, rage at the silliness of these instructions. (3)

Third, meditate, focus your ki, and kick the chest. (1)

Fourth, inspire courage among your companions by giving a shout. (7)

Fifth, recite your spelling list like you had in school. (8)

Sixth, sneakily attack the lock with a pick to open a secret compartment. (13)

Seventh, switch the wires to channel energy to the proper conduits. (1)

Eighth, now your gloves are probably in poor shape. Change them. (8)

Ninth, specialize in fighting with the absurdity of these directions. (2)

Tenth, learn that your favorite enemy is “confusing instructions”. (1)

Last, lay your hands on the chest, pray, and open it. (4)
