We input the answers to each puzzle titles into the completed bracket. We may then resolve the matches such that the victor of each round is the one that would win if they threw their associated tactic in a game of RPS. Fortunately, there are no ties.

As shown in the image, taking the nth letter of each answer, where n is the round the team finished in, gives us the message REF PASSED SECRETS, another RPS phrase.

Unfortunately, due to the corrupt actions of this referee, no prizes can be handed out this year.
