Solution: Duck Quonundrum

Written by Rahul Sridhar

This is a standard conundrum-style puzzle, with a small twist: the puzzle does not provide you with the 9 answers to start. Based on the constraints the operations impose, solvers should be able to determine that the nine answers are:

FLAT EXPANSE
FLYERS
GAME BIRD
LOCKS HORNS
MILKY WAY
NUMBER ONE SINGLE
OK CORRAL
PS AND QS
WHEATLEY

Once this has been determined, simply follow all the instructions and concatenate all the letters the puzzle asks you to "take" to get the answer FLAGRANT WATERFOWL. Once the conundrum is completed, the 9 entries should be the names of the 9 ducks in the Make Way for Ducklings picture book / statue.

Author's Notes

This was the last of the five metas to be written. We had 25 puzzle answers, but our meta meta required only 16 of them in order to use a symmetric bracket. We considered just telling teams which 9 teams were "disqualified", but we decided it would be nice to add another meta puzzle in which teams discovered this information for themselves. (This also preserved the nice property of releasing 5 puzzles every day).

So, we had to write a metapuzzle in which all the feeder answers were pre-determined, but the final answer was basically unconstrained. I really enjoyed solving the Coral Reef Meta from the 2015 MIT Mystery Hunt, and a conundrum-style meta provided plenty of freedom within our constraints, so I picked a thematic answer and came up with this.
