Solution: Identities

Written by Brian Chen

On the left is a log scale. Its bottom is 1 and each major tick mark is a power of 10. Minor tick marks are single-digit numbers times a power of 10.

Reading the letters in order of the numbers under the blanks gives the answer: ME, MYSELF, AND I.

Author's Notes

Some rejected matchings from this puzzle include 3 (singer) for BRITNEY SPEARS (rejected so we could have all distinct relations and I was not removing TAYLOR SWIFT since it provided the F), 2001 (director) for STANLEY KUBRICK (also rejected so we could have all distinct relations, because the film wasn't really called just "2001" but "2001: A Space Odyssey", and because the area around 2000 was already crowded), and 90210 (original creator) for DARREN STAR (having to disambiguate by specifying "original" was somewhat inelegant, and there are five series in the "Beverly Hills, 90210" franchise, with only the fourth really called "90210").

I had two numbers associated with Taylor Swift to make her unambiguous even without the enumeration, since most of the low integers have multiple songs named after them. Then I had both mathematicians associated with the same number 1729 to maintain cosmic balance.
